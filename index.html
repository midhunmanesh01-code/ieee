Who We Are

About IEEE SB CEC

IEEE Student Branch at College of Engineering Chengannur is a vibrant community of engineering students passionate about technology, innovation, and professional development. We organize workshops, hackathons, talks, and projects to bridge the gap between academia and industry.

🔭

Our Vision

To be a leading student community that fosters technological excellence and produces globally competent engineers ready to solve real-world challenges.

🚀

Our Mission

To provide students with opportunities to learn, collaborate, and innovate through technical events, industry exposure, and hands-on projects aligned with IEEE's global mission.

💡

Core Values

Innovation · Integrity · Collaboration · Inclusivity · Continuous Learning. We believe every student has the potential to make a meaningful impact.
